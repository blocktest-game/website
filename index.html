About

Blocktest is a small project aiming to bring the spark of open-source development and community input to a 2D sandbox game. Our developers have long wished for something which has elements from other games in the genre, while still remaining faithful to community input and contribution, and this is the culmination of that dream.

Blocktest first started in early 2021, and as of now is still in highly alpha development. We plan to have a fully functional release by the end of the year at earliest.
